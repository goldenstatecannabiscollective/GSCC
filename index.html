Plumas Farms

Plumas Farms is an indoor cannabis farm (ICF) that supplies Golden State Cannabis Collective. Located in Plumas County, CA, Plumas Farms is locally operated by members of the community. Plumas Farms is pioneering environmentally sustainable farming technology (Eco-Steady Tech) because we believe in a sustainable future.
